Selected works

Mexican Shabbat (2023-2024)

This project is an attempt to relate the practice of Shabbat with modern work rest. I was struck by the fact that work rest, seen as a ritual, contains almost all the characteristics of Shabbat or shares many similarities; what's different here is the purpose. In the creative process, I sought to portray a modern version of Shabbat -how it is lived- and took photographs almost exclusively on my rest days.

Post-Mexican Shabat (2024)

Unemployed, I decided to move the initial project from the realm of rest days to the absence of workdays, portraying leisure. For three months I took photographs whenever I felt like it, just seeking to create images from my free time.
